Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and later the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
Leading advocate for independence and later the 2nd President of the U.S.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned statesman, scientist, and diplomat who played a key role in securing French support during the Revolution.
Samuel Adams
Key organizer of the American Revolution and cousin of John Adams.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress at the time of the signing, known for his bold and large signature.
